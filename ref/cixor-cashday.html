Process Architecture Overview

Architecture
CIXOR CashDay (CCD) solves the SME liquidity timing gap by combining a compliant invoice engine, debtor verification, 90-day cash-flow forecasting, and a liquidation simulator into a single daily-use platform.

The Risk Waterfall

Invoice & Receivables
Deterministic, read-only calculation. Applies three layers to convert a Gross invoice amount into the maximum advanceable cash.
Layer 1 — Invoice Net-of-Tax Value
System-calculated. Taxes are pass-through obligations, never part of liquidation.
Gross − Tax
↓
Layer 2 — Eligible Liquidation Value (ELV)
Set by buyer at verification. Represents the undisputed, fully payable portion.
Net × (BuyerConfidence% / 100)
↓
Layer 3 — Invoice-to-Value Ratio (IVR) Haircut
Platform-level risk haircut set by CCD Admin. Stored in PlatformConfig.
ELV × (IVR% / 100)
↓
✓ Available to Liquidate
Maximum amount the seller may request for this invoice.
Net × (Confidence/100) × (IVR/100)
Available to Liquidate = (GrossAmount − TaxAmount) × (BuyerConfidencePercent / 100) × (IvrPercent / 100)

Cache Invalidation Triggers

Redis cache is immediately invalidated on: Invoice status change (Sent/Accepted/Rejected/Liquidated/Settled) · Expense created, updated, or deleted · Bank balance or overdraft updated · IVR configuration changed.

Liquidation Simulation

Cash & Expenses
Zero-commitment, iterative "what-if" analysis. Overlays a hypothetical cash-flow line on the forecast chart. No database writes during simulation.
Simulation State Machine
stateDiagram-v2 [*] --> Idle : Dashboard loaded Idle --> Simulating : User enters amount + Simulate Simulating --> Simulating : Adjust amounts / add-remove invoices Simulating --> Idle : Reset Simulation Simulating --> Confirming : Proceed to Liquidation Confirming --> Executing : Confirm & Request Liquidation Confirming --> Simulating : Back to simulation Executing --> [*] : LiquidityIntent created note right of Simulating : In-memory only\nRedis session TTL 15 min\nTarget response <200ms
1
User
Enters amount (0 < amount ≤ AvailableToLiquidate) in simulate field of a verified invoice.
2
Angular
Dispatches runSimulation action with {invoices: [{invoiceId, amount}]}.
3
Cash & Expenses
Retrieves cached base forecast from Redis. Computes total injection = Σ(requestedAmount) − CCD_Fee.
4
Cash & Expenses
Applies injection to base forecast at settlement date. In-memory arithmetic — zero database I/O.
5
Cash & Expenses
Stores simulation session in Redis (key sim:{companyId}:{sessionId}, TTL 15 min). Returns {actualForecast, hypotheticalForecast, totalInjection, totalFees, netInjection}.
6
Angular
Renders dashed cyan hypothetical line overlaid on forecast chart.

Glossary of Business Terms

Available Cash Today
BankBalance + (OverdraftLimit − OverdraftUsed). Day 0 starting point for the 90-day forecast.
Buyer Confidence %
Percentage of an invoice's net-of-tax value the buyer declares as undisputed and payable in full.
CCD Invoice ID
Unique human-readable identifier assigned to every invoice created in CashDay (e.g. CCD-2026-00123).
Debtor Officer
Individual at the buyer's organisation responsible for invoice acceptance and liquidation consent.
Eligible Liquidation Value (ELV)
Net-of-Tax Value × (BuyerConfidence% / 100). Portion eligible for liquidation after applying buyer confidence.
Invoice-to-Value Ratio (IVR)
Platform-level risk haircut applied by CCD to the ELV to determine the maximum advanceable amount.
Liquidity Intent
Record of the seller's confirmed request to liquidate a verified invoice, including a snapshot of all risk parameters.
Manual Receivable
Invoice entered into CCD for tracking purposes only. Always unverified and ineligible for liquidation.
Net-of-Tax Value
GrossAmount − TaxAmount. The economic value considered for liquidation (taxes are pass-through obligations).
System Receivable
Invoice created and sent through the CCD platform. Eligible for verification and liquidation.
Verified Receivable
Invoice accepted by buyer via OTP with buyer confidence % declared. IsVerified = true. Eligible for liquidation.
